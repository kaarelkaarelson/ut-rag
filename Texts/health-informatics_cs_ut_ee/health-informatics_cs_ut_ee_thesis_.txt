We offer a large number of areas/topics for BSc and MSc thesis related to our overall research directions to understand, design and mine the health data, as well as genetic and lifestyle risk factors. Many topics come with a potential to link students to existing research projects, i.e. with the potential of funding good and relevant work. A longer-term perspective is to also join the PhD programme at the Institute of Computer Science. The topics vary in terms of programming skills, medical and statistical background. If you are unsure what skills are exactly needed for the topic you are interested in OR you are interested in health informatics but did not find a suitable topic from those we have proposed OR you have your own research question you would like to address – just contact us and hopefully we will find a suitable thesis topic for you!
