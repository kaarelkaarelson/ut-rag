Our PhD student Adriano Augusto and his supervisor Marlon Dumas received the best paper award at the 36th International Conference on Conceptual Modeling (ER) held in Gifu, Japan, for their paper Automated Discovery of Structured Process Models: Discover Structured versus Discover and Structure . The paper presents a technique for automated discovery of block-structured process models from event logs produced by enterprise systems. The paper is co-authored with Raffaele Conforti and Marcello La Rosa from Queensland University of Technology.
