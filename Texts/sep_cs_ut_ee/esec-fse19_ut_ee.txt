Student Research Competition Committee Co-located Events Supporters Welcome to ESEC/FSE 2019 27th ACM Joint European Software Engineering Conference and Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering Tallinn, Estonia, 26-30 August 2019 Welcome to ESEC/FSE 2019 TALLINN, ESTONIA, 26-30 AUGUST 2019 The 27th ACM Joint European Software Engineering Conference and Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering (ESEC/FSE) is an internationally renowned forum for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, experiences, and challenges in the field of software engineering. The ESEC/FSE’19 program features six invited keynote talks, 98 research talks (including 24 journal-first talks), 21 industry talks, 15 tool demos, seven workshops, as well as a doctoral symposium, a student research competition, and several community events. Further information can be found in the ESEC/FSE’19 conference booklet . The conference will be held on 26-30 August 2019 in the conference centre of Sokos Hotel Viru in the modern city of Tallinn . Tallinn has a long history as an important trade centre in the Northern Baltic Sea region. Today, it is the vibrant capital of Estonia , the European leader in the development and promotion of an information society. ESEC/FSE’19  is organized by the Software Engineering and Information Systems Group of University of Tartu’s Institute of Computer Science , in collaboration with ACM and SIGSOFT. We look forward to a memorable edition of ESEC/FSE! Marlon Dumas and Dietmar Pfahl General Chairs Developed by Think Up Themes Ltd . Powered by WordPress .
