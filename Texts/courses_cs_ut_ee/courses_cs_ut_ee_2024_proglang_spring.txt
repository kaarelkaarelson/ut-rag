Time : Mondays 14:15-16:00. Place : Delta-2010 Language English. Contact : Kalmer Apinis. Zulip (log into courses to see link) The seminar is in the form of paper discussions. Requirements You need to choose a RECENT paper from PLDI or POPL. See POPL'22 , POPL'23 , POPL'24 , PLDI'22 , or PLDI'23 . Note that there are slides/videos of almost all POPL/PLDI talks. Choose an OLD paper (e.g., from RECENT-s references) that lays the foundation for the RECENT paper. Pick two available dates for your two required talks. (Register on Zulip!) Give a talk with slides on the OLD paper. Do not aim to be complete/reach the end of the paper. Give a talk with slides on the RECENT paper. Do not aim to be complete/reach the end of the paper. Approximate duration for talks: 35 min (<= 18 slides). A passing grade is given when the criteria above are satisfied.
