Registration to the seminar papers/slots is in Moodle. The seminars are held both on Wednesdays and Fridays at 16:15 on the following topics: 08.05.2024: Retrieval Augmented Generation 10.05.2024: Prompting 15.05.2024: Data Annotation 17.05.2024: Varia There are maximum 5 presentation slots in each seminar/topic. A selection of papers on each topic to choose from will be given. As there is max 20 presentation slots in total, the seminar work is expected to be done in groups of up to three people. For each presentation, the allocated time is 15 minutes: 10-12 minutes for the talk, 3-5 minutes for questions. Upload your presentation files in Moodle latest before the seminar to allow for easy access from the classroom computer. Õppematerjalide kasutamisel on kasutaja kohustatud viitama õppematerjalide autorile. Õppematerjalide kasutamine muudel eesmärkidel on lubatud ainult Tartu Ülikooli eelneval kirjalikul nõusolekul.
