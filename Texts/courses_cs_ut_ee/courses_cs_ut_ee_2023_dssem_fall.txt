Hajussüsteemide seminar - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 sügis Hajussüsteemide seminar (MTAT.08.024) EN Logi sisse Hajussüsteemide seminar 2023/24 sügis Pealeht Võimalike teemade loetelu MTAT.08.024 Hajussüsteemide seminar / Distributed Systems Seminar Time - WED 12:15 , weeks 2-16 Delta 2045 (&  Zoom (log into courses to see link) ) Seminar supervisors: Prof. Eero Vainikko, Dr Ulrich Norbisrath, Dr Amnir Hadachi, Dr Naveed Muhammad, Dr Stefan Kuhn, Artjom Lind, Mohammad Anagreh The seminar topics cover a broad set of themes on distributed systems (like High-Performance Computing, [Automatic] Parallelization, GPGPU, Peer-to-Peer systems,  (Grid and Cloud) Computing, parallel algorithms, parallel programming languages, geographic information systems and intelligent transportation systems, autonomous driving. In general, everything connected with distributed systems. Topic Areas (Concerned supervisors) : Parallel  Applications and Concurrent Computing (Eero Vainikko) Net Applications and Protocols (Artjom Lind) Applied Computer Vision (Artjom Lind) Parallel Machine learning algorithms (Artjom Lind, Amnir Hadachi) Geographic information systems (GIS) and Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) (Amnir Hadachi) GPGPU (Mohammad Anagreh) Autonomous driving (Naveed Muhammad) Students can develop a theme of their own related to Distribute Systems or choose a topic from the List of possible topics .
