Date Time Topic Type 14 Feb 2024 14.15-15.45 Introduction Seminar 21 Feb 14.15-17.45 Contemporary education Seminar 6 Mar 14.15-17.45 Self-regulated learning Seminar 20 Mar 14.15-17.45 Personalised education Seminar 03 Apr 14.15-17.45 Adaptive learning systems Seminar 17 Apr 14.15-15.45 Guest: AIEd & Ethics Seminar 24 Apr 14.15-17.45 ChatGPT and education Seminar 08 May 14.15-17.45 ChatGPT and education Workshop 22 May 14.15-17.45 Project presentations Seminar Process We use the flipped classroom method. Our seminars are discussion- and group-work-based, and students complete readings and assignments before the class. Each person must write a final report at the end of the course. Grading This is a pass/fail course, and no grades are given. You can miss only one of the in-person seminars to pass the course. The final report must be submitted on time.
