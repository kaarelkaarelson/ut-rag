Digitootejuhtimise seminar - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 sügis Digitootejuhtimise seminar (LTAT.05.022) EN Logi sisse Digitootejuhtimise seminar 2023/24 sügis Pealeht Objectives This course aims to give students an overview of, based on examples from different companies and research papers, different aspects that software product managers can face in their work. Learning outcomes On successful completion of this seminar, students will: be introduced to different aspects of digital product management practices; be introduced to how practitioners work with managing digital products; discuss topics related to digital product management processes. Teaching Team: Antti Ainamo, Sigrid Väljaru Brief description of content Session 1: Intro + user-centric design (lecture) 
Session 2: Tales from the Field (guest lecture) 
Session 3: Design Sprint (seminar) 
Session 4: Tales from the Field (guest lecture) 
Session 5: Artificial Intelligence (seminar) 
Session 6: Tales from the Field (guest lecture) 
Session 7: Digitalization and Organizational Leadership (seminar) 
Session 8: General discussion & course feedback (seminar) Assessment Non-differentiated (pass, fail, not present) Other information This course is offered in both Autumn and Spring semesters in two 3-ECTS modules. Students have the flexibility to complete one or both of the modules in any order of their liking. This course is part of Sandbox.
