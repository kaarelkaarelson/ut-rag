Luciano García-Bañuelos and Marlon Dumas received the best paper award at the 11th International Conference on Business Process Management held in Beijing, China, for their paper Slice, Mine and Dice: Complexity-Aware Automated Discovery of Business Process Models . The paper describes a technique for automatically discovering hierarchical business process models from large event logs. The paper is co-authored with Chatura Ekanayake and Marcello La Rosa from Queensland University of Technology.
