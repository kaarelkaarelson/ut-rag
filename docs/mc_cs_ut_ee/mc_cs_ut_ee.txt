Mobile & Cloud Lab conducts research and  teaching in the mobile  computing and cloud computing domains. Our  research topics include  cloud computing, mobile application development,  mobile cloud, mobile  web services, Internet of Things and migrating scientific computing  and  enterprise applications to the cloud. The group was founded by Prof. Dr. Satish Narayana Srirama .
