Äriprotsesside juhtimine - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Äriprotsesside juhtimine (MTAT.03.231) EN Logi sisse Äriprotsesside juhtimine 2023/24 kevad Home Lectures Practicals Assessment Submit Message Board Readings MTAT.03.231 Business Process Management Instructors Marlon Dumas (firstname.lastname ät ut dot ee) Anastasija Nikiforova (firstname.lastname ät ut dot ee) Eduard Ševtšenko (eduard.sevtsenko ät ut dot ee) For questions, please use the Slack workspace This course introduces key principles and methods of business process management along the entire lifecycle of business processes: from identification to redesign, automation and monitoring. The course emphasises the role of business process models as an instrument to  understand and analyse business operations, and to drive the design of Information Technology solutions that support the execution of business processes. The course combines business and IT methods and is designed both for IT and business students.
