Ettevõttesüsteemide integreerimine - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Ettevõttesüsteemide integreerimine (MTAT.03.229) EN Logi sisse Ettevõttesüsteemide integreerimine 2023/24 kevad Home Lectures Practicals Assignements Project and exam Message Board Enterprise System Integration - MTAT.03.229 Course Objective The objective of this course is to introduce principles and methods for designing, developing, and integrating enterprise information systems. The course introduces modern approaches to enterprise system integration, including service-oriented and Microservices architectures and their associated technologies. Teaching Staff Lecturer: Mohamad Gharib Supervisors of practical sessions: Iwada Eja Bassey and Viktoriia Abakumova Lectures & Practical session schedule Lectures: Mondays 14:15-16:00 - Delta Room 1019 Practical session: Tuesdays 14:15-16:00 ( Group 1 - Iwada Eja Bassey) and 16:15-18:00 ( Group 2 - Viktoriia Abakumova ) - Delta Room 1022 Formation of final result: There will be two assessment parts: 1. Assignments + Project (total 50 points). 1.1. Assignments (20 points): there will be three team assignments. 1.2. A project (30 points): students will develop in teams a software system that integrates multiple heterogeneous applications. The project will be assessed through several checkpoints. 2. A final exam (50 points). A grade of 20 points or less in the final exam will be mapped into a fail grade (F) . The final grade (out of 100) will be calculated as the sum of the points earned from the Assignments, Project, and the final exam . The final grade will be mapped to a grade between A and F using the standard University scale. A final grade of 50 points or less will be mapped into a fail grade (F) . A final grade of 51-60 points will be mapped into a grade (E) . A final grade of 61-70 points will be mapped into a grade (D) . A final grade of 71-80 points will be mapped into a grade (C) . A final grade of 81-90 points will be mapped into a grade (B) . A final grade of 91-100 points will be mapped into a grade (A) .
