Bioinformaatika - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Bioinformaatika (MTAT.03.239) EN Logi sisse Bioinformaatika 2023/24 kevad Main Schedule Assignments Introductory biology Projects Projects Key dates 14 June 2022 23:59 : Deadline for the final project report Project report Submit one PDF document by e-mail to kaur.alasoo@ut.ee The report should not exceed 3 pages in length (excluding figures). The report should contain at least the following components: Introduction. What was the question that you tried to answer? Why is it interesting? Why should other people care about your results? Is it a short or a long project? Methods. What approach did you choose? What were the exact steps that you decided to do? This should contain enough information for somebody else to reproduce your results. Results. What did you find out? Discussion. Did your approach work or not? What was challenging? What would you do differently next time? Author contribution. What was the role of each individual team member.
