Bioinformaatika seminar - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Bioinformaatika seminar (MTAT.03.242) EN Logi sisse Bioinformaatika seminar 2023/24 kevad Main Schedule Topics Tips Materials How to read a scientific paper? These articles might provide some useful tips: How to (seriously) read a scientific paper Presentations Please read your article thoroughly, review the cited sources if needed and try to see the bigger picture. Presentation should be around 15-20 min, hence it is not necessary to include all figures/graphs/tables.
