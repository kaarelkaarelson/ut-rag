Tõrketaluv kvantarvutus - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Tõrketaluv kvantarvutus (LTAT.00.015) EN Logi sisse Tõrketaluv kvantarvutus 2023/24 kevad Home LTAT.00.015 Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computing This 6 ECTS "book course" continues the studies in stabilizer theory, quantum error correcting codes and fault-tolerant quantum computing from the previous semester. Prerequisites Participants are expected to have a solid understanding of stabilizer theory and Gottesman-Knill theory, along with the mechanisms of the basic codes, including the rotated-qubits surface code (i.e., single-qubit Clifford gates, merge and split, rotation etc). Prerequisites will be checked in an oral exam.  Please contact the instructor. Meetings Tue 14-16 , starting from Feb 13. Room will be announced Credit Points Requirement for obtaining the 6 credit points are: Participation in the meetings Reading all discussed papers Presenting the content of one paper Instructor Dirk Oliver Theis Assoc.
