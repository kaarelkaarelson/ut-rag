3121 jaanika.seli@ut.ee +372 737 6426 The EIT Urban Mobility Master's programmes are two-year, double degree Master's programmes (120 ECTS) leading to two degrees, one from the Entry University and one from the Exit University as well as an EIT Label Certificate. The programme curricula include a mandatory minor in Innovation and Entrepreneurship of min. 30 ECTS, a mandatory MSc thesis of 30 ECTS and a mandatory organizational and international mobility. The programme Sustainable Urban Mobility Transitions (SUMT) focuses on the holistic approach in study, design, development and evaluation of sustainable, inclusive, resilience urban environment. Students will take part in an intensive summer school and a Kick-off event as part of the curriculum, focused on addressing critical city-based challenges. Visit EIT Urban Mobility Master School's website for more information. Structure of the 2-year programme: First year: Entry University, min. 60 ECTS including I&E courses (min. 18 ECTS) Summer School, 3 ECTS (included in second year I&E courses) Second year: Exit University, min. 60 ECTS including I&E courses (min. 12 ECTS) and situated industry-based Master's thesis (30 ECTS) Image Partner universities Aalto University – Espoo, Finland Eindhoven University of Technology – Eindhoven, Netherlands – exit year only KTH Royal Institute of Technology – Stockholm, Sweden Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC) – Barcelona, Spain University of Tartu (UT) – T artu, Estonia – exit year only University of Lisbon - Tecnico.IST – Lisbon, Portugal The programme is a comprehensive combination of an I&E Minor (30 ECTS) and a Technical Major (90 ECTS): 20-30 ECTS mandatory courses (‘common base’) MTAT.08.040 Intelligent Transportation Systems 6 ECTS LTAT.06.016 Mobility Modelling 6 ECTS MTAT.03.237 Practical Training in Informatics 6 ECTS (internship) 30-40 ECTS elective and specialized courses (‘specialisation’) The I&E spesialication courses: MTAT.03.310 Business Analysis 6 ECTS LTAT.05.011 Digital Product Design Introductory Project 3 ECTS The elective courses: LTOM.02.068 Evolution of Urban Structure 2 ECTS LTOM.02.041 Geospatial Analysis with Python and R 6 ECTS LTAT.06.014 Data Science for Urban Mobility 3 ECTS LTOM.02.063 Mobility Analysis for Planning 3 ECTS 30 ECTS master’s thesis project.
