Professionaalse enesearengu seminar - Kursused - Arvutiteaduse instituut Kursused 2023/24 kevad Professionaalse enesearengu seminar (LTAT.05.030) EN Logi sisse Professionaalse enesearengu seminar 2023/24 kevad Pealeht Loengud Viited Objectives The objective of the course is to support students participating in the Sandbox Programme and provide the necessary abilities for them to complete it. Description The seminars take place during two semesters. The topics covered in the seminars and the corresponding homework have been planned in a way that support the students during their participation in the Sandbox Programme and equip them with practical skills to manage their self-development during and after the program. Schedule Seminar 1 22.09.2023 kl 13.15-16.30 Introduction and goal setting, forming the group Seminar 2 26.10.2023 kl 12.15-15.45 Self-management and self-analysis Seminar 3 18.12.2023 kl 10.15-13.45 Time management, and focus Seminar 4 23.02.2024 kl 10.15-13.45 Stress management, and creativity Seminar 5 21.03.2024 kl 10.15-13.45 Factors of success and failure, taking responsibility Seminar 6 19.04.2023 kl 10.15-13.45 Feedback and self-assessment Learning outcomes A student that has taken the course has developed their understanding and gained practical skills in topics such as self-management and -analysis, time management and focusing, creativity, coping with stress. Assessment criteria This is non-differentiated (pass, fail, not present) course. The student has passed the course if they fill the following requirements: participated in all seminars submitted all homework tasks by the deadline all homework tasks correspond to the minimum requirements set The assessment criteria for each homework task are provided at the seminar with a detailed description of the task. Options for taking tests/exams at later date Possibilities for the overdue work submissions are decided case-by-case. This course is part of Sandbox.
